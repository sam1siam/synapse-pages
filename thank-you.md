# 🎉 Payment confirmed

Thanks for purchasing **Synapse for X**. Your license has been issued.

## What happens now

1. **Switch back to your Chrome browser** (the tab with this page can be closed).
2. Click the **Synapse** icon in your toolbar to open the side panel.
3. Go to **Options → 💎 Upgrade tab**.
4. The extension should auto-detect your payment within 60 seconds and activate your license. You'll see a `🎉 Activated` message.

## If it doesn't auto-activate

The side panel only polls for payment while the Upgrade tab is open. If you'd closed it during payment:

1. Open the Synapse side panel
2. Go to **Options → 💎 Upgrade**
3. Expand **"Have a license key already? Activate manually"**
4. Paste the license key from your email confirmation
5. Click **Activate license**

The license key was also delivered to the email address on file with NOWPayments.
